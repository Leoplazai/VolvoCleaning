Detailed Scope of Work

Standard Clean

Regular Cleaning (3 hours per visit):

  • Vacuum and mop floors in all areas
  • Dust surfaces in offices and common areas
  • Empty bins and replace liners
  • Clean and sanitize toilets and basins
  • General housekeeping in kitchen areas

Monthly Deep Cleaning (4 hours once a month):

  • Clean all windows and sills
  • High and low dusting
  • Deep clean kitchen appliances

Premium Clean

Includes all Standard Clean services, plus:

  • Wipe windows and sills during regular visits
  • Sanitize high-touch surfaces (doorknobs, switches)
  • Spot cleaning of walls and doors

Hyper Care Clean

Includes all Premium Clean services, plus:

  • Clean and polish glass surfaces
  • Sanitize telephones and keyboards
  • Clean inside microwaves after use
  • Monthly carpet steam cleaning
